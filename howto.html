How To Play

  • Press Space or click the pitch to jump over defenders.
  • Press Space twice quickly for a double jump to clear taller defenders.
  • Beat every defender in the match and the ball flies into the net. Goal means next level.
  • Get tackled and it is full time. You can replay the match.
  • Press P to pause during a match.
